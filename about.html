Why Choose Birchwood Workspace?

Working from Birchwood Workspace offers several benefits compared to working from home. Our workspace provides a professional environment that can help boost productivity and focus. With dedicated workstations, ergonomic furniture, high-speed internet, and meeting room, you can work efficiently without distractions. Additionally, being surrounded by like-minded individuals can foster creativity, collaboration, and networking opportunities. Utilising our workspace allows for a clear boundary between work and personal life, helping maintain a healthy work-life balance. Our offices offer a unique setting nestled in the rural beauty of Woldingham. Situated in close proximity to the M25, our location provides the perfect blend of tranquility and accessibility. Surrounded by picturesque landscapes, our workspace offers a peaceful and productive environment for all our clients. Whether you are coming from near or far, the immediate access to the M25 ensures a seamless journey to and from our offices, making it convenient for everyone. Our fully furnished office spaces include; Secure Offices Desks and Chairs Leased Line Internet with 200mbps circuit + Wifi (Up to 1000mbps) Individual Network per room with 8 port switch All Utility Bills Included Rubbish disposal/recycling Office cleans Site CCTV / Alarms Our Work Space is available on short or long term lets - Click on the “Workspace” tab to see our available space.
